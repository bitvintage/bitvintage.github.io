about bit.vintage


Bit.vintage is a small shop from the Pacific Northwest. During these trying times, starting a traditional retail shop is difficult, so we decided to provide that same superior in store experience, online, available directly in your home!

We take all our own photos, so the item you see, is the exact item you get! No stock photos, no reused photos, only high quality images for every unique item. We photograph every significant aspect of each item, giving you the same opportunity to evaluate and decide for yourself an item's condition, just like you could if you were browsing in a brick and mortar store!
